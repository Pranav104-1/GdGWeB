Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join GDG?

You can join our community by registering on our website. Simply fill out the registration form with your details, and you'll get access to all our events and resources.

Are events free to attend?

Yes! Most of our events are absolutely free for registered members. We believe in making quality education accessible to everyone in our community.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! We welcome developers of all skill levels - from beginners to experts. Our events are designed to cater to diverse experience levels.

How often are events held?

We typically host 4-8 events per month covering different topics. Check our events page for the latest schedule and upcoming sessions.

Can I volunteer with GDG?

Absolutely! We're always looking for passionate community members to help organize events. Contact us at events@gdg.com to learn more about volunteering.

How can I stay updated?

Subscribe to our newsletter, follow our social media channels, and enable notifications on our website to stay informed about upcoming events and announcements.
