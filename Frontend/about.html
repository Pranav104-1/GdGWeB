About Google Developer Group CSMU

Building communities at CSMU College. Fostering innovation. Empowering developers in Panvel.

Our Mission

Google Developer Groups are community groups for college and experienced developers interested in Google-developed technologies. By joining a GDG, you'll get the opportunity to interact with other developers in your community who are interested in Google technologies.

We believe that the best innovations happen when developers from diverse backgrounds collaborate, share knowledge, and inspire each other to create amazing things.
